CARVE

WAVE EDITOR · RAITO.STUDIO

A wave editor that runs entirely in your browser. The audio files you open are processed only on your own device — nothing is ever uploaded to or stored on a server. No install, no account.

📖 Read the user manual →

Features

Load WAV / AIFF / FLAC / MP3, range selection, fade in/out (5 curves), normalize, delete or extract a selection to a new file, loop playback, markers, WAV export (16/24-bit with TPDF dither, 32-bit float), and OGG Vorbis export (VBR quality control).
